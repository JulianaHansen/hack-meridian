    Description

    POAP Home is an application developed with POAP enthusiasts in mind, as the best way to enjoy your POAP collection on the go. Enjoy your own POAPs, browse your friend's collections, and get first-class support for POAP Moments to make vivid memories that last forever.

    - Explore Screen: You can now search across Collectors, Drops, and Collections!

    - Improvements: Enhanced deep linking, smoother UX/UI, better minting performance, plus multiple minor bug fixes.
